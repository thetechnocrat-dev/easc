Services

We build custom hardware and algorithms to facilitate bioelectricty, regenerative medicine, and developmental biology experiments.

We conduct scientific experiments to reproduce results in the planarian flatworm model organism.

Our goal is to provide scientists the tools to program bioelectrity. We believe the ability to coordinate large groups of cells will allow researchers to solve problems in tissue regeneration and age related functional decline.
